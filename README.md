TerraRose Farm Manager is a mobile-friendly web app designed to help manage a small commercial rose farm.

Features include:

• Sales tracking  
• Expense and receipt tracking  
• Labor hours and payments  
• Spray schedule planner and reminders  
• Irrigation calculator and irrigation logs  
• Profit reports and yearly tax summaries  
• Variety performance tracking  
• Farm performance dashboard  

The app is designed to work on a phone in the field and can be installed on the home screen like a regular app.

Created for TerraRose Farm in Providence, Ut
